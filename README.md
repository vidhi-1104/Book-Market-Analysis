Interactive Power BI Dashboard analyzing the Top 100 Best-seller books market, focusing on publisher performance and author sales volume.

# Top 100 Best-seller Books Market Analysis

## Project Overview
This Power BI project provides a deep-dive analysis into the UK's top-performing books. As a **Market Analyst**, the goal was to visualize sales concentration among authors and publishers to identify market leaders and genre trends.

Key Business Insights
* Sales Volume: The top 100 books represent a total market value of 150M.
* Market Concentration: A small group of authors, led by J.K. Rowling, dominates the volume sales, indicating high brand loyalty in the market.
* Genre Performance: General & Literary Fiction and Children's Fiction are the primary revenue drivers.
* Publisher Landscape: Bloomsbury and Random House maintain the highest market share by volume.

Technical Toolkit
* Power BI Desktop: Report authoring and data visualization.
* DAX: Created measures for volume percentages and market share rankings.
* Data Modeling: Cleaned and structured raw sales data for multi-dimensional analysis.
* Design: Implemented a professional monochromatic "Executive" theme for clarity and branding.
